About EcoTech Recyclers

Our Mission

We are dedicated to reducing electronic waste through responsible recycling and refurbishment practices.

Electronic waste (e-waste) is one of the fastest-growing waste streams worldwide. At EcoTech Recyclers, we provide comprehensive solutions for businesses and individuals to responsibly dispose of their electronic waste, ensuring that harmful materials are properly handled and valuable resources are recovered.

Our commitment to sustainable practices ensures that we minimize environmental impact while maximizing resource recovery. We believe that proper e-waste management is not just an environmental necessity but also an economic opportunity.

Proper E-Waste Disposal Methods

Recycling

Our state-of-the-art recycling process breaks down electronic devices into reusable materials like plastic, metal, and glass, which are then used to manufacture new products.

Refurbishment

When possible, we refurbish and repair electronic devices to extend their life cycle, reducing waste and providing affordable technology to those in need.

Secure Data Destruction

We ensure all data-bearing devices undergo secure data wiping or physical destruction to protect your sensitive information.

Certified Compliance

Our disposal methods comply with all local, state, and federal regulations, ensuring environmentally responsible handling of hazardous materials.

Environmental Impact

Improper e-waste disposal has severe consequences for our environment and health.

  • One recycled laptop saves enough energy to power a lamp for 40 hours
  • Recycling one million cell phones recovers 35,274 pounds of copper
  • E-waste contains toxic materials like lead, mercury, and cadmium
  • Only 17.4% of global e-waste is formally collected and recycled
